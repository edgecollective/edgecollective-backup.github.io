Edge Collective

Edge Collective promotes technological sovereignty for communities living at ‘the edge’: vulnerable cities, rural townships, farming communities, islands.

We facilitate workshops, trainings, and collaborative development that focus on building locally-owned, open-source, municipal and community infrastructure.

Recent projects include resilient telecommunication, off-grid water monitoring, and open source retrofitting of 'defunct' proprietary greenhouse control systems.
