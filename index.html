Edge Collective

Edge Collective promotes technological sovereignty for communities living at ‘the edge’: vulnerable cities, rural townships, farming communities, islands.

We facilitate workshops, trainings, and collaborative development focused on building locally-owned, free and open-source municipal and community infrastructure.

Recent projects include resilient telecommunication, off-grid water monitoring, and retrofitting of greenhouse control systems.

You can view all of our designs and code on github.

If you'd like to contact us:
